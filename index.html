Hello! I am a final-year M.S. student in computer science at Columbia University, where I am incredibly fortunate to be advised by Alexandr Andoni and Mitya Chklovskii. My research interests lie in the algorithmic foundations of massive data, theoretical computer science, and high-dimensional probability, including algorithm design for problems in high-dimensional computational geometry, metric embeddings, graph algorithms, algorithms for learning and inference in graphical models, and algorithms for sampling from high-dimensional distributions. Previously, I graduated with my B.A. from Columbia University, where I studied computer science and mathematics.
