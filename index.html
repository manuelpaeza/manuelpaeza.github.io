Hello! I am a first-year M.S. student in computer science at Columbia University, where I am incredibly fortunate to be advised by Alexandr Andoni and Mitya Chklovskii. My research interests lie in the algorithmic foundations of massive data including algorithm design for problems in high-dimensional computational geometry, metric embeddings, graph algorithms, as well as algorithms with provable guarantees for problems in machine learning and designing neural computation models. Previously, I graduated with my B.A. from Columbia University, where I studied computer science and mathematics.
